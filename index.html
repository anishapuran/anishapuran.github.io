Travel to Singapore

Singapore is a safe and beautiful country, suitable for all people.

The picture above shows The Merlion, the official mascot of singapore. Located at merlion park, this beautiful statue is a great tourist attraction with a mall and starbucks nearby. next to the statue is a great body of water perfect for instagram worthy pictures.

The Marina Bay Sands is a hotel, more specifically the best hotel in asia. The top of the sands holds a spectacular pool and view of Singapore.

The Jewel, located at changi airport is the perfect way to end your singapore trip. The inside is just as beautiul if not more than the exterior. Inside you will find $1.7 billion dollars worth of stores, food, and lush trees and bushes and a magestic waterfall that continues to flow down all the levels of the jewel.

Singapore is a place that at times, seems to be a land out of a fairytale. A place that really goes hand in hand with sentence previous is Gardens By the Bay. The gardens are a certainly a sight for sore eyes and an unforgetable experience. When daylights gone, the gardens are lit beautifully, a truly magical scene.
